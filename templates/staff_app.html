How Staff Use It

  1. Install the app on an Android phone.
  2. Sign in with the same Brownberries staff email and password.
  3. Allow fine location, background location, and notifications.
  4. Leave monitoring enabled while working.
  5. The app checks position roughly every minute and syncs attendance automatically.

If a staff member leaves the cafe geofence, the app starts the checkout grace timer. If internet is unavailable, it waits longer and syncs the checkout when the phone reconnects.
